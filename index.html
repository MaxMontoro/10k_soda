The Magic of Soda

An alternative for sugary soft drinks

Soda water is heaven on Earth.
Refreshing, cold, clear and sparkling,
it makes your day wonderful, darling.
Say: "No more!" to all that soft drink,
You are hard-core enough, I'm sure, I think.
You don not need no sweetener neither.
( Triple negation is my coolest feature. )
It is large and cheap, hell, be my guest!
Just pay for these, I'll repay, I guess.
In case you've been always wondering
How carbon dioxide dissolves in a drink,
Look at the equation here below
And have a glass of water,
my good old fellow.

H2O(l) + CO2(g) ⇌ H2CO3(aq)
